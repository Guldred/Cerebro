# Employee Handbook

## Working hours and flex time

Standard contracts are 40 hours per week with core hours from 10:00 to 15:00.
Outside core hours, employees arrange their schedule with their team lead.
Flex-time balances are settled quarterly.

## Vacation policy

Full-time employees receive 30 vacation days per year. Requests go through the
HR portal and need team-lead approval at least two weeks in advance for
absences longer than three days.

## Equipment

Every employee receives a laptop and a peripherals budget of 500 EUR,
refreshed every three years. Equipment requests go through the IT service
desk.
