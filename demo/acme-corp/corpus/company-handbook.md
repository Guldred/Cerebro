# Acme Robotics — Company Handbook

Welcome to Acme Robotics. This handbook is public to all employees and covers the
basics everyone needs on day one.

## About us

Acme Robotics was founded in 2019 and is headquartered in Munich, Germany, with a
second office in Lisbon. We build warehouse automation software. The company runs a
four-day-week pilot every August.

## Working hours and time off

Core collaboration hours are 10:00–16:00 CET. Employees accrue 30 days of paid leave
per year. Public holidays follow the Bavarian calendar.

## Who to ask

People questions go to the People team in the `#people` channel. IT support is in
`#it-help`. Anything you cannot find here is probably in the team-specific spaces —
ask your manager for access.
