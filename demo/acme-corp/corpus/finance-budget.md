# Q3 Budget — RESTRICTED (Finance only)

Visible only to the Finance group. The codename and figures below appear in **no other
document**.

## Project Northwind

Project Northwind — the new Lisbon fulfilment-centre rollout — has an approved Q3
budget of €2.4M. Of that, €1.1M is capital expenditure on conveyor hardware and €0.6M
is headcount for six new platform engineers.

## Controls

Spend above €50,000 requires a second signature from the Finance lead. Quarterly
re-forecasts are due on the last Friday of each quarter.
